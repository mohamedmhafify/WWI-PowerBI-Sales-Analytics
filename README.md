# 📊 Wide World Importers - Power BI Sales Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-End Power BI Business Intelligence Report analyzing the 
**Wide World Importers (WWI)** distribution dataset (26,397 sales records).

Built as part of the **NTI Creativa Data Analytics Diploma**
at Cairo University Hub | 2026.

---

## 🗄️ Data Model
- **Star Schema** with 1 Fact table (FactSale) and 5 Dimension tables
- Full Power Query cleaning: renamed columns, fixed data types, 
  handled corrupted values
- Custom **ExtractedColor** feature using an 871-color lookup table

---

## 📐 DAX Measures Library (24+ Measures)

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Core Sales & Profit | Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin % |
| Time Intelligence | Sales YoY %, Sales MTD, Sales YTD |
| Fiscal Calendar | Sales Fiscal YTD, Profit Fiscal YTD |
| Rankings | Customer Sales Rank, Product Sales Rank |
| Customer Analysis | Repeat Customer Rate, Distinct Customers |

---

## 📄 Report Pages

1. **Executive Overview** — KPIs, monthly sales trend, YTD vs Fiscal YTD
2. **Customer & Geography Insights** — Treemap by state, Top 10 customers
3. **Salesforce & Product Performance** — Employee performance, Scatter analysis
4. **Strategic Insights & Recommendations** — Key business takeaways

---

## 🔑 Key Business Insights

- 💰 **$19.88M** Total Sales | **49.92%** Profit Margin
- 📍 **California** alone drives **~45%** of total sales
- 🏆 Top salesperson outperforms team average by **11.7%**
- 📦 Just **10 core products** contribute **34.7%** of total profit
- 👥 Top **20%** of customers generate **~48%** of total profit
